About me


Hello, and welcome to my website! My name is Joey Lujan and I am a web developer. I've been around computers ever since I was a little kid, and while growing up I was always curious about how computers worked and how people were able to create cool and useful things with programming. After starting college, I noticed there was a major in web development, so I decided to enroll in the first class to see how it was. And, to my surprise, I immediately fell in love with it. I found that web development satisfies the side of me that likes to problem solve, and the side of me that likes to create and design the look of projects. While enrolled in college, I started doing some freelance work with CMS software like Wordpress, while also doing some work that involved developing websites from scratch or going into the files for websites that were already built and tweaking some code.
